Slam Sports

Project information

  • Category: database/JS
  • Project date: 20 August 2020

Data visualize project of a sports website that displays a dataset containing statistics on the 2018 World Cup. A web app that takes the data and uses it in a variety of ways,a row of buttons that show the top countries by goals scored, goals against and a map of all the countries that participated in the World Cup. Includes: Multiple different types of graphs or layouts,easy to read and understand,legends, axis, labels,with good use of styling and colours,click events,transitions and mouseovers etc.
